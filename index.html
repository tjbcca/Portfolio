About Me

I'm a software developer from Mississippi, and graduate of Base Camp Coding Academy. Ever since I was young I was fascinated with computers and when coding was first introduced to me I would spend days working on simple projects with beginner block code. Though I stopped that during High School, Base Camp reignited my interest in coding and allowed me to learn more varied and advanced languages. I enjoy both the logical and creative aspect of code and there is nothing more satisfying than seeing things come together as intended. I am also a graduate of Itawamba Community College, with an Associate of Arts.
